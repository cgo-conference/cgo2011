Social Event at Montenvers - sponsored by Google

Tuesday, April 5th
17:00     Leaving from Congress Center, going by foot to Montenvers train station.
17:30     Train leaves for Montenvers site (no other train, be on time !).
18:00     Train arrives at Montenvers; beautiful sight of Chamonix glacier called "La Mer de Glace" (also, you can take a peek at the Crystal Gallery and the Museum of Alpina, close by).
18:45     Cocktail in the restaurant or on the terrace (weather permitting), followed by dinner (tartiflette: cheese-based local specialty; alternative vegetarian dishes available).
21:00     First train returns to Chamonix.
22:00     Second train returns to Chamonix.

After 20 minutes by train along the mountain and the alpine wood, you will discover the fabulous site of Montenvers - Mer de Glace. Have a look at the glacier, famous mountains around before the dinner in hotel du Montenvers.

The Montenvers Hotel, mythical place of the Chamonix Valley, was built in 1880. Well renovated, the restaurant will welcome you in a warmly mountain atmosphere for an unforgetable evening.

The Restaurant at the Montenvers can accommodate up to 200 people. Social event participation will be granted on a first come first served basis.
